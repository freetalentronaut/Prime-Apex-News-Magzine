About Prime Apex News Magazine

Prime Apex News Magazine, a collaboration between First August Solutions Limited and Olu Angel Ventures, was founded in October 2021 by media practitioners Ola Jones (Editor-in-Chief) and Angela Daodu (Managing Director). Our inaugural issue was published on November 11, 2021.

Mission

To deliver timely, accurate, and impactful news that captivates and informs our audience while setting the pace for frontline journalism in Nigeria and beyond.

Vision

To position Prime Apex as a leading and respected news medium globally, known for journalistic excellence and captivating storytelling.

Our Story

Initially launched as a monthly news magazine circulated nationwide through subscriptions, Prime Apex expanded two years later with the launch of Prime Apex Glitz — an event & lifestyle magazine featuring influential Nigerians of taste. Our editorial team is well-trained and committed to versatile, accurate reporting that resonates globally.
